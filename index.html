My Hair Journey

Growing up with thick 4C hair was a challenge: tangles, shrinkage, and frizz made haircare overwhelming. I wanted a space to help Black women embrace their natural hair, learn what works, and feel empowered. That’s how Hey Curlfriend was born!

About Hey Curlfriend

  • Take a quiz to discover your hair type (2C → 4C)
  • Get personalized product recommendations
  • Follow haircare routines, including bonnets
  • Learn protective hairstyles
  • Keep your crown healthy, moisturized, and thriving

Hair Types

2C

Loose S-shaped waves. Frizz easily. Lightweight products work best. Take the quiz to see if you’re 2C!

3A

Defined, springy curls. Hydrating products enhance shine and curl shape.

3B

Medium curls with volume. Extra moisture reduces frizz. Protective styles help length retention.

3C

Dense, corkscrew curls. Rich creams, leave-in conditioners, and gentle detangling are key.

4A

Soft S-shaped coils. Retain moisture well. Love twists and braids.

4B

Z-shaped coils. Needs moisture and gentle handling. Protective hairstyles prevent breakage.

4C

Tightest coils. Highly fragile. Protective styling, deep conditioning, and gentle care are essential.
