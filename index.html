Hair Types

2C - Wavy Hair

Loose S-shaped waves. Hair tends to frizz easily and may need lightweight styling products. Take our hair type quiz to see if youâ€™re 2C!

3A - Loose Curls

Defined, springy curls with a slight shine. Best cared for with hydrating products and curl-enhancing routines.

3B - Medium Curls

Voluminous curls that may need extra moisture and curl creams to reduce frizz. Protective styles work well for length retention.

3C - Tight Curls

Dense, springy curls that form corkscrews. Benefit from rich creams, leave-in conditioners, and careful detangling.

4A - Soft Coils

Well-defined S-shaped coils. Retain moisture well with oils and butters, love protective styles like twists and braids.

4B - Z-shaped Coils

Hair bends sharply in a zig-zag pattern. Needs gentle handling, moisture-rich products, and protective hairstyles to prevent breakage.

4C - Tightest Coils

Very tight, dense coils. Highly fragile and shrinkage-prone. Protective styling, deep conditioning, and gentle care are essential. Hey Curlfriend helps you figure out routines perfect for 4C hair!
